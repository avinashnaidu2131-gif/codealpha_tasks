# PHISHING AWARENESS QUIZ

## Question 1
You receive an email saying your account will be blocked in 1 hour. What should you do?  
A) Click the link immediately  
B) Ignore it  
C) Check the sender and visit the official website manually  
D) Reply to the email  

Correct Answer: C

---

## Question 2
Which of the following is a sign of a phishing website?  
A) HTTPS lock icon  
B) Misspelled domain name  
C) Clean design  
D) Company logo  

Correct Answer: B

---

## Question 3
What is social engineering?  
A) Hacking using software  
B) Trick people into giving information  
C) Fixing security systems  
D) Programming networks  

Correct Answer: B

---

## Question 4
What should you do if you clicked a phishing link?  
A) Do nothing  
B) Restart your device  
C) Change passwords and report it  
D) Share it with friends  

Correct Answer: C
